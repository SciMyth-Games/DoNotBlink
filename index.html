§ 01

Introduction

Welcome to Don't Blink ("the Game"), developed by SciMyth Studio.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you download or play the Game on your Android device. By downloading or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

§ 02

Age Restrictions

The Game is intended for users aged 13 years and older.

The Game may contain:

  • Horror themes
  • Suspenseful gameplay
  • Mild frightening content
Children under 13 should play only with parental guidance where permitted by local laws.
§ 03

Information We Collect

The developer of Don't Blink does not directly collect personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Government identification numbers
However, third-party advertising services embedded in the Game may automatically collect certain technical and device-related information as described in Section 4.
§ 04

Advertising & Monetization

The Game may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

Data collected by advertising services

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location information
  • App usage information
  • Performance and diagnostic data

How this data is used

  • Deliver relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertisement performance
  • Improve ad relevance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
§ 05

Use of User Data

Information collected through advertising and analytics services may be used to:

  • Provide advertisements within the Game
  • Improve game performance and stability
  • Diagnose crashes and technical issues
  • Analyze gameplay trends
  • Improve overall user experience
The developer does not sell personal information to any third party.
§ 06

Data Sharing

The developer may share information only with trusted third-party service providers required for the operation of the Game, including:

  • Advertising providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Platform providers (Google Play)

These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies. The developer does not share data beyond what is necessary for the Game's operation.

§ 07

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect information associated with the Game.

No method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
§ 08

User Access & Controls

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to:

  • Reset your advertising identifier
  • Limit ad personalization
  • Manage privacy settings through Android device settings
  • Control app permissions through device settings

These controls can be found in your Android device's Settings → Privacy or Settings → Ads menus.

§ 10

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact the developer immediately using the contact information in Section 13 so that appropriate action can be taken.
§ 11

International Users

The Game may be distributed worldwide through Google Play. By using the Game, users understand that information processed by third-party service providers may be transferred to and stored in countries outside their country of residence, where different data protection laws may apply.

§ 12

Changes to This Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

Any changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
